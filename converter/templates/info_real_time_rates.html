What Are Real-Time Rates?

Real-time exchange rates are the current prices at which one currency can be exchanged for another. These rates fluctuate continuously throughout the trading day based on supply and demand in the foreign exchange market (Forex). A real-time rate typically reflects prices updated every few seconds to minutes, providing the most accurate conversion information available.

Our system updates exchange rates every 24 hours from reliable APIs, ensuring you have the most current information for your currency conversions. This frequent updating means you can trust the rates displayed for accurate financial transactions and comparisons.

1
